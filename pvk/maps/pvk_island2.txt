PVK_ISLAND2
-===========-

Game Type: Booty

Knights: Pirates landed ashore and the Vikings in the near by
town both want to get there hands on the Royal Treasure.
Defend it at all costs! and guard the aqua ducts near by.

Vikings: To satisfy your greed for Treasure, forcefully take
the Royal Chest and bring it back to your respected areas.

Pirates: Your ship sunk and you need some gold in order to get
a new one built. Fight or steal the nearby Royal Treasure and bring
it back to your little island.
