PVK_Skull Strategy Guide

With the implementation of Pilliage Mode into the PVK code, Skull was converted from a Booty map (where you gather and hoard chests) to a Pilliage map (where you break things for fun and profit.) This map has the knights franticly defending their base from the invading Pirates and Vikings.

PVK_Skull

Objectives:

The Knights try and protect the cross and the throne from destruction at the hands of the Pirates and Vikings. The default round time limit is five minutes. If the Knights manage to keep at least one objective intact when time exipires, they win. Attackers get points for the objectives that they destroy.

Map Overview:

The Knights' base consists of a huge skull errupting from a small, tree covered, island. A Pirate Caravel is on one side of the island, and a Viking Longship is on the opposite side. A dense ring of trees surrounds the skull. There are two paths through the forest, one by each ship. There is a main enterance in the neck of the skull. Two tall ladders on either side of the skull allow acess to narrow ledges high on the skull. There is an underwater tunnel that leads from the ocean nearest the top of the skull to the basement inside the skull.

The main enterance, ladders, and tunnel are the only ways in to the skull. Be aware of your options when attacking. If the knights set up a strong defense in one area, attack another route of entry. Knights would do well to prevent enterance into their base. These enterances can act as choke points to focus murderous fire upon attackers. Each route gives a defender a nice, clean, shot with a crossbow or longbow.

The layout of the skull's interior consists of five areas:

Basement

The underwater tunnel leads into this dank room. Ramps lead up into the chapel.

Chapel

The main enterance leads to a bridge that crosses a pool of lava, beyond this is the chapel. The chapel houses a cross made of marble which is one of the two objectives on this map. Ramps lead down to the basement and up to the middle landing.

Middle Landing

This is a well traveled area, as it leads to many important parts of the Knight's base. Ramps lead down into the chapel. A single ramp leads up to the Knight's respawn. A ladder leads up to the ledges on the outside of the skull. Two other ramps lead into the throne room.

Knights' Respawn

Ammo, armor, and food for the Knights is here. A metal grate blocks the way for unsavory Pirates and Vikings from entering this area. There are two windows that overlook the throne room here.

Throne Room

Two ramps lead down to the middle landing. The roof is open, attackers can drop in from the ledges from outside to attack the throne. The throne is isolated on ground suspended over a pool of lava. The throne is the other objective on this map.

Tips and Strategy:

Communicate with your team members. Teamwork is essential, moreso on defense.

The Knights dont stand a chance against the combined might of the Pirates and Vikings. However the Pirates and Vikings ARE NOT ALLIES, in fact they are competing with each other to destroy what the Knights hold dear. It is in the best interest of the Knights to get the Pirates and Vikings to waste their time and energy fighting each other.

Pirates and Vikings are competing against each other, so if you encounter someone from the other team, it is in your best interest to kill them if you think they are going to make it to an objective before you. If you think you can reach an objective before your enemy, and not get caught between any defending Knights and your enemy, who is also after the same goal, then avoid your enemy and go for the goal.

Missle weapons and kegs are much less effective on the objectives than melee weapons. It will take a month of Sundays to destroy them with the pistols, muskets, thrown axes or spears. Close in and smite them with hammer, sword, and axe.

The round time limit can be adjusted by the server in the pvk_skull.cfg file in the root pvk directory.
