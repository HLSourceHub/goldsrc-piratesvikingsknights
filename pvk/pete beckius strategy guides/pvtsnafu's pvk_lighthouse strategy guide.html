PVK_Lighthouse Strategy Guide

Lighthouse is not a complicated map. This overview is designed to help newer players understand what is going on, and why they get cussed out by their teammates when they wander off with a captured chest. Some strategic insights may be gained by more experienced PVK players.

PVK_Lighthouse

The Knights try and defend a quaint oceanside town by a lighthouse sitting on a precarious cliff. The local businesses have grown rich with booty. The Knights must keep this treasure out of the Pirate and Viking fortified camps, just outside of town. Pirates and Vikings seek to increase their wealth by placing as much booty as possible within their camps.

Map Overview:

The lighthouse stands tall in a corner of the map. It is the Knights' respawn area and serves as a strategic place for archers to rain death upon attacking foes. Two rows of buildings seperate the lighthouse from the forest. The five businesses are in the town, they are noted in the picture below. The enterances to the Pirate and Viking camps are in the forest.

Each business is protected by a stout wooden door. It will take many hits with a melee weapon to break them in. Missle weapons and powder kegs are ineffective on the doors. Use your melee weapons. Each business contains useful things. The tanner has leather armor. The armorer has chainmail and platemail. The weaponsmith has arrows, crossbow bolts, throwing axes, and javelins. The gunsmith has pistol and shot. The tavern has food. If this wasn't enough incentive to attack the town, each business has a treasure chest.

The Pirate and Viking camps are protected by barriers. The barriers have team specific gates that keep they enemy out. There is a weak part of the barrier that can be broken down. Inside the camp is a pallasade. This also has a team specific gate on it. On the back side of the pallasade is a weak spot similar to the barrier that can be broken in. The Pirates and Vikings will get points for any booty that is beyond the team markers just outside the barrier. You do not even have to get the booty past the first barrier to score points. It would be more secure if it were placed in the pallasade, for obvious reasons. Both the barrier and pallasade have battlements that can be manned.

Tips and Strategy:

Communicate with your team members. Teamwork is essential to coordinate attack and defense.

Try and keep your barriers from being broken. If they are intact, then your enemies can't steal your booty. Pirates and Vikings should keep at least one team member back to man the battlements and defend their barrier and pallasade from being broken in to. Knights should try their best to defend the doors to the businesses.

If your booty is taken, do your best to keep it out of the hands of the enemy. The Knights will get points for any treasure not in a Pirate of Viking scoring zone.

From the lighthouse, archers can see and defend all five doors. Get the range on the spot just outside each door. When an enemy attacks that door, you can hit him with one well placed shot. Archers take the first shot with the crossbow, as it is more powerful than the bow. Switch to the bow so you can fire faster and continue to rain arrows on the enemy. Be wary of any Sniper Pirates. Up high in the lighthouse, they are your only threat.

Stay with and cover the any teammates carrying booty. They can't fight and move slow while carrying booty.

The round time is 15 minutes by default. It can be changed by altering the pvk_lighthouse.cfg file in the pvk root directory.
