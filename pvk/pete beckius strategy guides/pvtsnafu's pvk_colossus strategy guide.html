PVK_Colossus Strategy Guide

Hello, I am PvtSnafu, the guy who made PVK_Colossus. I have observed some people were having problems understanding the map. So I am writing this to help out the newbies. After reading this you will know where to go and how to get there and what should you do to help your team win. Here we go:

PVK_Colossus

Objective:

Territorial Control. Get as many of your teammates into the colossus' head and have them stay there as long as possible. For every teammate you have in the head for a certain period of time (5-10 seconds) you get a point.

Secondary Objective:

Control the blimp. You do not get any points for controlling the blimp. There is a cannon on the blimp that can blow up everyone in the head if you manage to fire it into the head. No one can hold the head for long unless they also control the cannon on the blimp. The cannon takes 20 seconds to reload.

When you respawn, you will find yourself in a safe (assuming friendly fire is off) area to collect your thoughts, ammo, and armor before you bravely jump through the portal into the fray. Should you ever get confused about what teleporter takes you where there is a greyish "booth" or information center that you can step into. Once you step into the information center messages will appear on your screen telling you the objectives of the map and what teleporters you should use to get where you want to go.

If you are STILL confused check out my handy Portal Guide

Bottom Level: Colossus and Blimp

Once you step throught the first portal you will be dropped in to crotch deep water underfoot of a giant bronze statue chasing a blimp. For those of you who have never played Colossus before, you will probably need a second or two to take it all in. Ok there is no advantage to being underfoot running around in the water here. The first thing you should do is run to the green castle shaped portal that the colossus is towing behind it, or jump on the island with the palm tree on it, if you see it.

The island will seperate from the ground and fly around the colossus two times before flying by the blimp. Consider the island as an alternative way of getting on the blimp. Remember the blimp is a secondary objective. There is another (easier) way on to the blimp. Inside the blimp is a red monolith shaped portal that will take you to stonehenge, should you decide to jump through it.

Middle Level: Castle, Tavern, and Wizard's Tower

When you step through the green portal on the bottom level, you will find youself in the courtyard of a castle. Inside the keep of the castle you will find ammunition and armor for all of the teams. Walk out of the front gate of the castle and you will see a four-way drawbridge. To progress to the colossus you must cross all four bridges. The bridges will open and close regularly so that you can proceed straight across to the other side. Do not get caught by the four-way drawbridge. If you do you will be killed. A few seconds before the drawbridges close and the other two open, you will see lightning sparking inside the four-way drawbridge.

After crossing the first two bridges, you will be standing in front of a gallows. Take a left and cross a land bridge and you will find yourself in front of a tavern. Inside the tavern, you will find food. You can eat the food if you are injured to heal yourself. Once the drawbridges switch, you can cross over them and you will be standing in front of a white tower with a green portal in front of it. This is the Wizard's Tower. Walk through the green smoky portal and you will appear inside the tower.

Inside the tower you will see two reddish portals. They open and close at regular intervals in time with the opening and closing of the drawbridges. Walk through them when they are open. These portals will take you to stonehenge. If you look up you will see two other levels in the Wizard's Tower. You can get to these other levels from inside the Colossus and from Stonehenge. On the middle level of the tower, there is a distorted red lava portal that is an alternate way into the Colossus. From the top level of the Wizard's Tower you can see the entire Middle Level map from a bird' s eye view. You can also see down into the tower and drop down onto the second or first level.

Top Level: Stonehenge

Stonehenge is a central jump off point to critical parts of the map. There are five portals here. Like the two portals in the Wizard's Tower, they open and close in time with the drawbridges. The middle red lava portal will take you inside the Colossus and it is the primary route into the Colossus. There are two purple portals here as well. These portals will take you to the blimp, and they are the primary route into the blimp. Each purple portal will take you to a different part of the blimp. There are two green portals here as well. these will take you back to the Wizard's Tower. They will deposit you on the top level of the Wizard's tower.

Strategy:

Communicate with your team members. Teamwork is essential to controlling both the Colossus and the Blimp.

If your team holds the Colossus and the blimp, set up a defense on the Middle Level and try to prevent the other two teams from making it to the Wizard's Tower. Sniper Pirates can take shots at the enemy from the top of the tower without having to worry about the enemy closing in with melee weapons.

There are other strategic points you can control if you have a lot of people playing. The land bridge on the Middle Level. Stonehenge.

The flying island on the Bottom Level should be considered an alternative way of getting to the blimp. It is difficult to get on and it is not always present to use. Anyone on the blimp can use the cannon to blast you off of the island.
